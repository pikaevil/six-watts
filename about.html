About Six Watts

Six Watts was founded with a singular focus: to bridge the gap in after-sales service for the booming Electric Vehicle industry. We specialize in EV charger repair, installation, and advanced diagnostics for 2-wheelers and 3-wheelers across South India.

Mission

To deliver fast, reliable, and accurate EV charger services that ensure uninterrupted operations for fleets and OEMs. We aim to minimize downtime through precision engineering.

Vision

To become South India’s most trusted EV service partner, contributing to cleaner mobility by ensuring charging infrastructure is always online and efficient.

Our Story

The EV revolution in India faced a critical challenge: a lack of specialized repair centers for chargers. Most manufacturers had to rely on expensive replacements rather than cost-effective repairs.

Recognizing this gap, Six Watts established a dedicated facility in Bangalore. Starting with basic repairs, we quickly evolved into a high-tech diagnostic center capable of handling complex PCB reworks, CAN bus analysis, and firmware recovery. Today, we are the authorized service partner for major OEMs and fleet operators.
