Our Services

Comprehensive charger & vehicle electronics services tailored for OEMs, fleets and service centers.

EV Charger Repair

We repair AC and DC chargers ranging fro 2kW to 11kW and beyond. Our lab is equipped to handle:

  • Power Module Failures (Mosfet/IGBT replacement)
  • Control Card Logic Repairs
  • Communication Errors (OCPP/CAN)
  • Connector & internal wiring fixes
  • Safety interlock troubleshooting

Installation & Commissioning

Proper installation is key to charger longevity. We provide end-to-end site setup:

  • Site feasibility & load survey
  • Earthing & cabling validation (Megger tests)
  • Installation of MCBs/RCCBs and protection devices
  • Signage & bollard installation
  • Handover training for site staff

Controller & Cluster Repair

Beyond chargers, we service vehicle electronics for major 2W/3W OEMs:

  • Motor Controller (MCU) Power Stage rework
  • Instrument Cluster display replacement
  • Firmware updates & parameter flashing
  • Throttle/Brake sensor calibration
  • Water damage recovery

Maintenance (AMC)

Prevent downtime with our comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts:

  • Quarterly on-site preventive health checks
  • Filter cleaning & cooling fan inspection
  • Connector wear-and-tear analysis
  • Software/Firmware OTA update support
  • Priority SLA for breakdown calls

Our Service Process

1. Pickup / Inttake

We pick up the faulty unit or you drop it at our center. A Job Card is created instantly.

2. Diagnostics

Our lab engineers trace the fault using advanced tools. You get a report & quote.

3. Repair

Approval received. We replace components, re-solder, and fix the issue.

4. Load Test & Dispatch

Unit undergoes load testing. Once passed, it's packed and shipped back.
